Carlos Antonio Ribeiro Duarte

Computational Fluid Dynamics expert.

Carlos Duarte is an assistant professor of mechanical engineering at UFCAT. He holds a doctoral degree, a master's degree and a B.S. in mechanical engineering from Federal University of Uberlândia. Duarte's research spans basic and applied research for multiphase fluid flows and aero/hydrodynamics using computational fluid dynamics. His research interests include multiphase flows, turbulence, and large-scale simulations.
